About this playbook

🌏 How to use this playbook ▼

Think of this space like a neighborhood library where no one shushes you: wander in, pull random books off the shelf, flip through whatever sparks joy, leave the rest exactly where they are.

Everything here is meant to be skimmed, borrowed, annotated, remixed, and taken home in your metaphorical tote bag. There's no correct route. Just follow what feels useful to you.

🔓 Open-source, because gatekeeping is boring ▼

Everything here is free, accessible, and made to be passed around. Screenshots welcome. Copy-paste encouraged. If it helps someone spark a conversation or start a tiny climate uprising with their cousins, perfect.

🥥 Rooted in Southeast Asia ▼

Born out of real conversations in the Philippines, Thailand, and Indonesia. It speaks our languages, our humor, our chaos, and our ways of taking care of each other. It's glocal — sweaty humidity and traffic jams baked in.

🪴 FAQs ▼

Can I copy parts of this? Yes, please. Copy, paste, remix — whatever helps you and your community.

I'm outside Southeast Asia. Is this for me? Totally. Just know it was built from lived experiences here. Adapt as needed.

What if I'm overwhelmed? Same. Take one page at a time. Or close the tab, drink water, and return when your neurons feel less toasted.
